One-Pan Sausage Ricotta Lasagna

This recipe is packed with flavor, comes together in under an hour, and only uses one pan for easy cleanup!
Plus, it's naturally vegetarian and can easily be made vegan with a few substitutions.



Here's what you'll need:

For the Sausage Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Make the Sausage Sauce: Heat olive oil in a large ovenproof skillet over medium heat.
    Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds more.
  3. Add sausage and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper.
    Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Assemble the Lasagna: Spread 1/3 of the sausage sauce on the bottom of the skillet. Top with 4 lasagna noodles.
  6. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and parsley. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    Top with another 1/3 of the sausage sauce.
  7. Repeat layers with 4 more noodles, remaining ricotta mixture, and 1/3 of the sauce.
  8. Top with remaining noodles and sauce. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the top.
  9. Cover the skillet tightly with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  10. Uncover and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Tips:

  • For a richer flavor, you can use mascarpone cheese instead of ricotta cheese.
  • If you don't have fresh parsley, you can use 1 teaspoon dried parsley.
  • To make this dish vegetarian, use plant-based sausage crumbles instead of Italian sausage.
  • To make this dish vegan, use vegan ricotta cheese and nutritional yeast instead of Parmesan cheese.

I hope you enjoy this easy and delicious lasagna recipe!
