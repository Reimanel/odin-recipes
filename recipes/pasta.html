Creamy Lemon Ricotta Pasta with Asparagus and Peas

I've got the perfect recipe for you: Creamy Lemon Ricotta Pasta with Asparagus and Peas.
It's packed with fresh flavors, comes together in under 30 minutes, and only uses one pan!
Plus, it's naturally vegetarian and can easily be made vegan with a few substitutions.



Here's what you'll need:

  • 12 oz(340g) box of your favorite pasta (penne, farfalle, or orecchiette are all great choices)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium shallot, thinly sliced
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the shallot and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add the asparagus and peas to the skillet and cook until tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired.

Tips:

  • For a richer flavor, you can use mascarpone cheese instead of ricotta cheese.
  • If you don't have fresh lemon juice, you can use bottled lemon juice, but be sure to taste the sauce before adding it, as bottled lemon juice can be more tart.
  • To make this dish vegan, use vegan ricotta cheese and nutritional yeast instead of Parmesan cheese.

I hope you enjoy this easy and delicious pasta recipe!
